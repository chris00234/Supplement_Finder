GNC Women’s GNC WOMEN’s PRENATAL FORMULA WITH IRON: Provides Nutritional Support for Mom & Baby
Item # 214312

The GNC Women's Prenatal Formula with Iron provides nutritional support for Mom & Baby before, during, & after pregnancy.  This product features 1000mcg of folic acid to support fetal development and is required for cell growth and DNA formation* .  This product also includes 600mg of calcium for bone support and 18mg of Iron to support fetal needs and a mother's increased blood volume*.

  • Provides nutrients for mom & baby
  • For women before, during & after pregnancy
  • Supports fetal development*
  • Provides bone health support*

This product is eligible for FSA and HSA plans. Restrictions may apply based on your individual insurance plan. Contact your insurance provider about plan allowances and requirements. While FSA/HSA cards cannot be accepted, please use a credit card or debit card to pay for your eligible items and then submit the receipt to your plan based on the current policies for reimbursement.
